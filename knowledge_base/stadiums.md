# IPL Stadiums

## Wankhede Stadium

Wankhede Stadium is located in Mumbai, Maharashtra.
It is the home ground of Mumbai Indians (MI).
Capacity: approximately 33,000 spectators.
The stadium has hosted many IPL finals and important matches.
It was the venue for India's 2011 Cricket World Cup final victory.

## M. A. Chidambaram Stadium

M. A. Chidambaram Stadium is located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.
It is the home ground of Chennai Super Kings (CSK).
Capacity: approximately 50,000 spectators.
Also called Chepauk Stadium.
One of India's oldest cricket grounds.

## Eden Gardens

Eden Gardens is located in Kolkata, West Bengal.
It is the home ground of Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR).
Capacity: approximately 66,000 spectators — one of the largest cricket grounds in the world.
Famous for its passionate cricket-loving crowd and electric atmosphere.

## Narendra Modi Stadium

Narendra Modi Stadium is located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat.
It is the world's largest cricket stadium with a capacity of 132,000 spectators.
It is the home ground of Gujarat Titans (GT).
Formerly called Sardar Patel Stadium and Motera Stadium.
The stadium hosted the 2023 ICC Cricket World Cup final.

## M. Chinnaswamy Stadium

M. Chinnaswamy Stadium is located in Bengaluru, Karnataka.
It is the home ground of Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB).
Capacity: approximately 40,000 spectators.
Known for its short boundaries making it a high-scoring venue in IPL.
