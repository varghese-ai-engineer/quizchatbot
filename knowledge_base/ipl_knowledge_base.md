# IPL Knowledge Base

## IPL Overview

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India.
It was founded in 2008 by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI).
IPL uses the T20 format where each team faces 20 overs.
Each IPL season consists of league matches followed by playoffs and a grand final.
IPL is one of the most watched cricket tournaments in the world.

## IPL Format and Rules

IPL Basics:
- Each team plays 14 league matches
- Top 4 teams qualify for playoffs
- Qualifiers 1 and 2 determine the finalists
- The Eliminator removes the 3rd-ranked team
- The Grand Final decides the champion
