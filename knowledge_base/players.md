# Famous IPL Players

## MS Dhoni

MS Dhoni is known as Captain Cool.
He is the captain of Chennai Super Kings (CSK).
Dhoni is famous for his calm finishing and wicketkeeping skills.
IPL titles as captain: 5 (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023).
Total IPL runs: 5000+. Highest score: 84*.
He is considered the greatest IPL captain of all time.

## Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is the highest run scorer in IPL history.
He plays for Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB).
Total IPL runs: 8000+.
He is the only player to score 4 IPL centuries in a single season (2016).
IPL Orange Cap winner multiple times.

## Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is a five-time IPL-winning captain.
He captained Mumbai Indians (MI) to 5 IPL titles.
Known for his elegant batting and powerful hitting.
Total IPL runs: 6000+.

## Chris Gayle

Chris Gayle is the six-hitting king of IPL.
He has hit the most sixes in IPL history — more than 358 sixes.
His highest IPL score is 175 not out off 66 balls for RCB against Pune Warriors India in 2013.
He hit 17 sixes in that single innings — an IPL record.
He scored a century in just 30 balls — the fastest IPL century.
Gayle has scored 6 centuries in IPL — the most by any player.
He is nicknamed the "Universe Boss" for his destructive six-hitting ability.
Total IPL runs: 4900+.

## AB de Villiers

AB de Villiers is known for 360-degree batting.
He plays for Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB).
Known for his extraordinary shot selection and power hitting.
He is among the top six-hitters in IPL history.
Highest IPL score: 133* off 59 balls.

## Jasprit Bumrah

Jasprit Bumrah is one of the best IPL fast bowlers.
He plays for Mumbai Indians (MI).
Renowned for his yorkers and death-over bowling.
IPL wickets: 150+.

## Suresh Raina

Suresh Raina is known as Mr. IPL.
He played for Chennai Super Kings (CSK).
Total IPL runs: 5528. Highest score: 100*.
Strike rate: 136+.
He is the first player to score a century for India in all three formats of cricket.

## Yuzvendra Chahal

Yuzvendra Chahal is the highest wicket-taker in IPL history.
He plays for Rajasthan Royals (RR).
Total IPL wickets: 200+.
He is a leg-spin bowler known for his variations and googly.

## Lasith Malinga

Lasith Malinga is famous for his yorker deliveries.
He played for Mumbai Indians (MI).
IPL wickets: 170+.
He is a Purple Cap winner and one of the most feared death bowlers in IPL.

## David Warner

David Warner is a prolific IPL opener.
He played for Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) and now Delhi Capitals.
He won the Orange Cap multiple times.
He led SRH to the IPL title in 2016.
Total IPL runs: 6000+.
