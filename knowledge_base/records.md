# IPL Records

## Batting Records

Most runs in IPL history: Virat Kohli (8000+ runs)
Highest individual score: Chris Gayle scored 175* (not out) off 66 balls for RCB in 2013.
Second highest score: Brendon McCullum scored 158* in the first ever IPL match in 2008.
Most centuries in IPL: Chris Gayle with 6 centuries.
Fastest century: Chris Gayle scored a century in 30 balls.

## Six-Hitting Records

Most sixes in IPL history: Chris Gayle has hit the most sixes in IPL history with 358+ sixes.
Second most sixes: AB de Villiers is among the top six-hitters in IPL history.
Most sixes in a single innings: Chris Gayle hit 17 sixes in his 175* knock against PWI in 2013.
Most sixes in a season: Chris Gayle holds the record for most sixes in a single IPL season.
Chris Gayle is known as the "Universe Boss" for his explosive six-hitting ability in IPL.

## Bowling Records

Most wickets in IPL history: Yuzvendra Chahal with 200+ wickets.
Best bowling figures in a single match: Alzarri Joseph took 6 wickets for 12 runs (6/12) on IPL debut.
Most wickets in a season: Harshal Patel took 32 wickets in IPL 2021.

## Team Records

Highest team score in IPL: Sunrisers Hyderabad scored 277 runs against Mumbai Indians.
Lowest team score in IPL: Royals Challengers Bangalore scored 49 runs.
Most IPL titles: Chennai Super Kings and Mumbai Indians each with 5 titles.

## Fastest Fifties

Fastest fifty in IPL: Yashasvi Jaiswal scored a fifty in 13 balls.
KL Rahul scored a fifty in 14 balls.
Pat Cummins scored a fifty in 14 balls.

## Partnership Records

Highest partnership in IPL: Chris Gayle and Virat Kohli put on a massive partnership for RCB.
Chris Gayle has been involved in many record-breaking IPL partnerships.
