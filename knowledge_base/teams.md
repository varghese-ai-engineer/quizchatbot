# IPL Teams

## Chennai Super Kings

Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is one of the most successful IPL teams.
Captain: MS Dhoni, known as Captain Cool.
IPL titles: 5 (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023).
Home ground: M. A. Chidambaram Stadium, Chennai.
Famous players: MS Dhoni, Suresh Raina, Ravindra Jadeja, Dwayne Bravo, Ruturaj Gaikwad.

## Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians (MI) is a five-time IPL champion team based in Mumbai.
Captain: Rohit Sharma (5 IPL titles as captain) and now Hardik Pandya.
IPL titles: 5 (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020).
Home ground: Wankhede Stadium, Mumbai.
Famous players: Rohit Sharma, Jasprit Bumrah, Hardik Pandya, Lasith Malinga.

## Royal Challengers Bengaluru

Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) is a popular IPL team despite no title yet.
Captain: Virat Kohli (most seasons) and Faf du Plessis.
Home ground: M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bengaluru.
Famous players: Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, Chris Gayle, Glenn Maxwell.

## Kolkata Knight Riders

Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) has won the IPL title 3 times.
IPL titles: 3 (2012, 2014, 2024).
Captain: Gautam Gambhir (2012, 2014), Shreyas Iyer (2024).
Home ground: Eden Gardens, Kolkata.
Famous players: Gautam Gambhir, Andre Russell, Sunil Narine.

## Rajasthan Royals

Rajasthan Royals (RR) won the first ever IPL title in 2008.
IPL titles: 1 (2008).
Captain: Shane Warne (2008).
Home ground: Sawai Mansingh Stadium, Jaipur.
Famous players: Shane Warne, Sanju Samson, Ravichandran Ashwin, Jos Buttler.

## Sunrisers Hyderabad

Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) won the IPL title in 2016.
IPL titles: 1 (2016).
Captain: David Warner (2016 winning season).
Home ground: Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium, Hyderabad.
Famous players: David Warner, Kane Williamson, Bhuvneshwar Kumar.

## Delhi Capitals

Delhi Capitals (DC) were earlier called Delhi Daredevils until 2019.
The team has not won an IPL title yet.
Home ground: Arun Jaitley Stadium, Delhi.
Famous players: Rishabh Pant, Axar Patel, David Warner.

## Punjab Kings

Punjab Kings (PBKS) were earlier called Kings XI Punjab until 2020.
The team has not won an IPL title yet.
Home ground: PCA Stadium, Mohali.
Famous players: Chris Gayle, KL Rahul, Jonny Bairstow.

## Lucknow Super Giants

Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) is a new IPL franchise that debuted in 2022.
The team has not won an IPL title yet.
Captain: KL Rahul.
Home ground: BRSABV Ekana Cricket Stadium, Lucknow.

## Gujarat Titans

Gujarat Titans (GT) is a new IPL franchise that won the title in their debut season 2022.
IPL titles: 1 (2022).
Captain: Hardik Pandya (2022 winning season), Shubman Gill.
Home ground: Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad.
