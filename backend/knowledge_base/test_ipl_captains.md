# IPL Captains

## Most Successful Captains

MS Dhoni is the most successful captain in IPL history, having led Chennai Super Kings (CSK) to 5 IPL titles in 2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, and 2023. He is known as "Captain Cool" for his calm leadership style.

Rohit Sharma captained Mumbai Indians (MI) to 5 IPL titles in 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, and 2020, making MI the most successful franchise overall.

## Current Captains

- CSK: Ruturaj Gaikwad (took over captaincy from MS Dhoni in 2024)
- MI: Hardik Pandya (returned to MI and took captaincy in 2024)
- RCB: Faf du Plessis (captain since 2022)
- KKR: Shreyas Iyer (led KKR to 2024 IPL title)

## Youngest Captain

Sanju Samson is the captain of Rajasthan Royals (RR). Pat Cummins captains Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH).

## Captain Records

Rohit Sharma holds the record for most IPL titles won as a captain with 5 championships. MS Dhoni is close behind with 5 titles as well, making them joint record holders.
